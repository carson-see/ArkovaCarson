# services/worker/scripts/intelligence-dataset/scenarios/ferpa

FERPA training scenarios covering the operational heart of education privacy law (K-12 and postsecondary).

## Files

- `ferpa-core.ts` — Core scenarios across 10 categories: consent, directory-info, disclosure-exceptions, access-amendment, disclosure-log, emergency-disclosure, state-overlay, enforcement, hipaa-boundary, vendor-contracts. Citations from `sources/ferpa-sources.ts`.
- `ferpa-advanced.ts` — Advanced/edge-case FERPA scenarios.

## Constraints

- All citations must use canonical IDs from `sources/ferpa-sources.ts`.
