## Kenya Credential Types

- **KNEC** — Kenya National Examinations Council (KCPE, KCSE certificates)
- **TSC** — Teachers Service Commission (teaching licenses)
- **KMPDC** — Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council
- **LSK** — Law Society of Kenya (advocate licenses)
- **ODPC** — Data Protection Commissioner (compliance certificates)

## Australia Credential Types

- **AHPRA** — Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (medical/nursing/allied health)
- **TEQSA** — Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency (higher ed registration)
- **ASQA** — Australian Skills Quality Authority (VET sector accreditation)
- **ACNC** — Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission
- **CPA Australia** / **CA ANZ** — Accounting certifications
