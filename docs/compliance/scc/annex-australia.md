# SCC Annex — Australian Privacy Act 1988

> **Parent:** `base-template.md` | **Jira:** SCRUM-573 (REG-12)

## Applicable Law
Privacy Act 1988 (Cth); Schedule 1 Australian Privacy Principles (APPs); Part IIIC Notifiable Data Breaches scheme; Privacy and Other Legislation Amendment Act 2024.

## Supervisory Authority
Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) — https://www.oaic.gov.au

## Cross-Border Transfer Basis
APP 8 (Cross-border disclosure): Entity must take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipient handles information in accordance with the APPs.

Under Section 16C, Arkova remains **legally liable** for overseas recipient's handling of Australian personal information. These SCCs, combined with the APP 8 assessment (REG-17), provide the contractual framework.

## Jurisdiction-Specific Requirements

### APP 8 Obligations
- Take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipient complies with APPs
- Contractual provisions must require compliance with APP-equivalent protections
- Entity is liable for acts/omissions of overseas recipient as if they were its own
- Ongoing monitoring obligation (not a one-time assessment)

### Data Subject Rights (APPs 12-13)
| Right | APP | Arkova Implementation |
|-------|-----|----------------------|
| Access to personal information | APP 12 | Self-service data export |
| Correction of personal information | APP 13 | Data correction request form (REG-19) |

### Notifiable Data Breach (Part IIIC)
- **30-day assessment window** from awareness of grounds to suspect eligible data breach
- Notification to OAIC if assessment concludes eligible data breach occurred
- Notification to affected individuals: as soon as practicable after assessment
- Template: OAIC online notification form (NDB scheme)

### Penalties (Privacy and Other Legislation Amendment Act 2024)
- Serious or repeated interference: up to **AUD 50,000,000** or 30% of adjusted turnover or 3x benefit obtained (whichever is greatest)
- This represents the highest penalty exposure of all Arkova-served jurisdictions

### Special Categories
- Health information receives heightened protection under APP 3 (collection limitations) and APP 6 (use and disclosure limitations)
- Tax file numbers: not processed by Arkova
