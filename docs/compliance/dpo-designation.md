# Data Protection Officer / Information Officer Designation

**REG-28 (SCRUM-589)** | Effective: April 2026

## Designated Officer

**Contact:** privacy@arkova.ai

The DPO/Information Officer serves as the single point of contact for data protection inquiries across all African jurisdictions where Arkova operates.

## Jurisdiction-Specific Responsibilities

### Kenya (Data Protection Act 2019)
- Primary liaison with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)
- Ensure ODPC registration is current
- Handle data subject access requests per Sections 25-38
- Report data breaches to ODPC within 72 hours
- Maintain records of processing activities

### South Africa (POPIA)
- Registered Information Officer with the Information Regulator
- Handle data subject requests under Sections 23-24
- Ensure Section 72 compliance for cross-border transfers
- Report security compromises as soon as reasonably possible
- Maintain PAIA Section 51 manual

### Nigeria (NDPA 2023)
- Primary liaison with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)
- Handle data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
- Report data breaches to NDPC within 72 hours
- Ensure compliance with cross-border transfer requirements (Section 43)

## Annual Training Requirements

- Annual refresher on each jurisdiction's regulatory updates
- Review of data subject request handling procedures
- Update to breach notification procedures
- Review of cross-border transfer mechanisms (SCCs)

## Contact in Privacy Notices

The DPO email (privacy@arkova.ai) is displayed in all jurisdiction-specific privacy notices for Kenya, South Africa, and Nigeria via the `JurisdictionPrivacyNotices` component.
